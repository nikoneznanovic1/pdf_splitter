Privacy Policy

At PDF Hub, we value your privacy. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Cookies

This website uses cookies to enhance user experience and for advertising and analytics purposes. Cookies are small text files stored in your browser to remember your preferences and usage behavior.

2. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your previous visits to this or other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings.

3. Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to collect aggregated information about website traffic and user interactions. This data helps us improve our services and user experience.

Data collected may include anonymized IP addresses, browser types, operating system, device types, referral sources, and pages visited. These data are not personally identifiable.

Google Analytics sets cookies to track usage behavior. These cookies are non-essential and are only activated after obtaining your consent.

You can opt out by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. File Processing

Uploaded PDF files are processed temporarily and automatically deleted shortly after processing. We do not store your files permanently or access their content.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to outside parties, except as required by law or as needed to operate the site (e.g., Google services).

6. Your Consent

By using this site, you consent to our use of cookies, Google Analytics, and Google AdSense as described above.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or privacy concerns, feel free to contact us at info@pdfhub.org.
