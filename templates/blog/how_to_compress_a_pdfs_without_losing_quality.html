Why Compress a PDF?

Large PDF files are difficult to email, upload to websites, or store in cloud systems. Compressing a PDF solves these problems by reducing file size through optimization techniques such as image scaling, metadata cleanup, and font subsetting.

However, not all compression tools are equal. Low-quality tools may degrade image clarity or break layout structures. That's why it's important to choose a tool that prioritizes quality retention — like PDFHub.
