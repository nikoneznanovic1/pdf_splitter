Need to separate specific pages from a large PDF file? This guide will show you how to split a PDF online for free, using the best tools available in 2025.

Why Split a PDF?

Splitting a PDF is useful when you only need certain pages from a large document. Instead of sharing the entire file, you can extract the relevant sections and create smaller, more manageable PDFs.

Best Free PDF Split Tools in 2025

  • PDFHub.org: Quick and secure splitting with drag-and-drop functionality.
  • SmallPDF: User-friendly interface for extracting single or multiple pages.
  • ILovePDF: Allows both page range selection and splitting into multiple files.
  • PDF2Go: Great online platform for splitting and editing PDFs.

Pro Tips for Splitting PDFs
